## Name & Subtitle

**Name:** Loomi
**Subtitle:** Stress Relief & Calm

## Description

Loomi is a companion-led wellness app designed to make stress relief feel
approachable and personal. Through guided breathing, grounding exercises, and
daily mood check-ins, Loomi helps users pause, reset, and build healthier
emotional habits in just a few minutes a day.
